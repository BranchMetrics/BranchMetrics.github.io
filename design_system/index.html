Hide/shows should be a WRAPPER (parent) classes to prevent conflicts with elements

  • bds-hide
  • bds-hide-at-s
  • bds-hide-at-md
  • bds-hide-at-lg
  • bds-hide-at-xl
Showing:

  • bds-show-at-s
  • bds-show-at-md
  • bds-show-at-lg
  • bds-show-at-xl
Examples
reize screen or view mobile emulator to see examples

How do you show a message on mobile but hide on desktop?
Solution: class="bds-hide-at-lg"

How do you hide a message on mobile, but show on desktop?
Solution: class="bds-hide bds-show-at-lg"

bds-order-* changes the order in which your content shows up.
If you take a look at the html, you'll notice that the green bds-container is declared BEFORE the orange bds-container, but when its rendered on the page, the green bds-container shows up AFTER the orange.

class="bds-order-1"
Here is an example where the order changes on desktop but not on mobile. Resize your screen or use an emulator to see the difference.
*note: always set up your content in mobile hierarchy, then use css to change desktop.
